About Us

Welcome to VijayLakshmi Traders

Vijayalakshmi traders Since 1989, we are wholesale delears in varieties of Rice, Pulses, Atta, Maida, Sugar, Sooji, Oil, Chilli, Jagery, Salt and Spices, from all over South India. We are wholesale grocer, also a wholesale grocery distributor , we play a critical role in the supply chain of food and grocery products. we purchase goods in large quantities from manufacturers or producers and then distribute them to retailers, foodservice providers, and other businesses that sell groceries to the end consumers. Our commitment is to provide you the best quality groceries and at best price. We establish relationships with various manufacturers, producers, and suppliers of grocery products. Providing at most customer service is essential for us. We address customer inquiries, concerns, and issues promptly.
